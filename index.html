About us

In 2020, Lindt & Sprüngli celebrated its 175th anniversary. As a global leader in the premium chocolate sector, the company looks back on a long-standing tradition with its origins in Zurich, Switzerland. Today, quality chocolate products by Lindt & Sprüngli are made at 12 of its own production sites in Europe and the USA. They are distributed by 32 subsidiary companies and branch offices, in about 500 own stores, and also via a comprehensive network of more than 100 independent distributors around the globe. With over 14,600 employees, the Lindt & Sprüngli Group reported sales worth CHF 4.97 billion in 2022.
